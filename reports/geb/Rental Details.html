Description

The Space

With a 20 foot ceiling, this loft provides amazing light and has an airy feel to it. The wall of windows offer beautiful views of Madison Square Park and the Empire State Building during any season. With all of the amenities, you can either enjoy time at home watching the city, or step out the front door and be in the heart of the city!

Guest Access

You have full use of the entire home during your stay. The home has wireless internet, cable TV, and there is laundry in the building

Interaction with Guests

I am happy to help you with any suggestions of things to do while you are in town. I am a native New Yorker and know all of the fun and off the beaten path things NYC has to offer!

The Neighborhood

The Flatiron District is home to wonderful shopping and restaurants, including Eataly, Almond, and CraftBar. The Highline and Union Square are within a 10 minute walk. With Chelsea to the West, the Village to the South, Herald Square to the North, and Gramercy Park to the East the apartment is an ideal place to stay!

Getting around

The home is located within 1 block of 2 major lines, and is therefore very easy to access via public transportation and from all major airports. There is parking available within walking distance.

I visited Stacey's apartment with my mum and sister for 5 nights. We had booked a long time in advance, because when I saw this apartment I knew it would be a good one for our stay. Like others have said the narrow stairway is a bit tricky for getting suitcases up, but once you are in the apartment and see the view it is extremely worth it.

It is in a great location overlooking the park. We usually walked, but the subway links are great. We were also using the New York pass, and there is a bus stop for the tour just across the road on 5th Avenue.

The apartment was very clean and well decorated, with everything that you would need in the kitchen. The sofa bed has changed from the pictures shown, and as I am 6'4 it was a little short for me, but still very comfortable to sleep on. There were lots of towels and sheets provided as well. Everything was thought of, including a large selection of New York maps and guides. Stacey was also just a text message away for any queries.

Thanks for making the trip memorable and I would recommend anyone going to New York to consider Airbnb, and specifically this apartment!
